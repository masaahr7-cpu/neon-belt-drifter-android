# Privacy Policy for Neon Belt Drifter

Effective date: July 10, 2026

Neon Belt Drifter is a single-player arcade game. The game does not ask players to create an account and does not intentionally collect personal information.

## Data Stored on Device

The game stores the player’s best score locally so it can be shown again in future sessions.

## Network Use

The app may load game audio or artwork assets from the app bundle or from the configured asset URLs used by the game build. If hosted platform leaderboard services are enabled, the final score may be submitted as a number only.

## Children’s Privacy

Neon Belt Drifter does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Third-Party Services

If you publish through Google Play, Google Play and Android system services may process device, install, crash, purchase, or analytics data according to Google’s policies. This app does not include advertising SDKs.

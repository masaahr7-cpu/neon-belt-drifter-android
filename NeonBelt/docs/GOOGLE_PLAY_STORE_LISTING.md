## Short description

## Full description
Drift through a glowing retro asteroid belt in fast, satisfying arcade runs built for mobile.

Drag anywhere to guide your neon starfighter, squeeze through dangerous gaps, collect energy shards, and build a streak multiplier before the belt overwhelms you. Every run is quick, readable, and instantly replayable.

Features:
- Mobile-first drag controls
- Escalating asteroid pressure
- Energy shard streak scoring
- Saved high score
- One-tap replay
- Synthwave visuals, particles, music, and impact feedback

## About text
Neon Belt Drifter is a compact mobile arcade dodger about precision, rhythm, and one-more-run score chasing. It is designed for short sessions and quick retries.
